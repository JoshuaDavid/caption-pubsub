Pub / Sub Captions

This is a very basic system for allowing one person to write captions and many other people to read them. To get started, head over to the Publish Stream page. On that page, you will find a link titled View Stream, which you can distribute to all of the people who want to view that stream, and a textarea in which you can enter text. Enter text in that box, and everyone who is viewing the stream will see what you've entered.

Have fun!
